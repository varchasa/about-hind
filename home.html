HISTORY OF INDIA


INDUS VALLEY CIVILISATION

  1. Dayaram sahni first discovered harappa in 1921.
  2. RD Banerjee discovered mohenjodaro in 1922.
  3. Covered parts of punjab, sindh, baluchistan, gujarat,rajasthan and wastern UP.
  4. Barter system was there. 16 was the unit of measurement.

Vedic Period : The Aryans

  1. The aryans were semi nomadic pastoral people and originated from sea around the caspian sea in central asia
  2. The central asian theory is given by max muller.Entered india through Khyber Pass around 1500 BC.

Early Vedic or Rig Vedic Period(1500-1000 BC)

  1. Monarchial form. Tribe was known as Jan and its king as Rajan.

Later Vedic Period : (1000-600 BC)

  1. The four fold division of society introduced - Brahmins(priests), Kshatriyas(Warriors),Vaishyas(Agriculturists, cattle rearers),Shudras(servers of upper three).
  2. The gotra appeared for the first time in this age.
